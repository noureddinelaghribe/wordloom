Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the WriteFlow app

Can I use WriteFlow without an internet connection?

Some features of WriteFlow, such as basic text editing and offline saving, can be used without an internet connection. However, advanced features like AI paraphrasing, grammar checking, and content generation require an active internet connection to access our cloud-based AI models.

Is WriteFlow available on iOS devices?

Currently, WriteFlow is only available for Android devices. We are actively working on developing an iOS version and plan to release it in the near future. Stay tuned for updates!

How accurate is the AI Detector?

Our AI Detector uses advanced algorithms to analyze text patterns and identify characteristics commonly found in AI-generated content. While it is highly accurate, no AI detection tool is 100% foolproof. It should be used as a helpful guide rather than a definitive judgment.

Does WriteFlow store my data?

We prioritize your privacy and data security. WriteFlow processes your text on our secure servers only for the purpose of providing the requested features (e.g., paraphrasing, grammar checking). We do not store your content after processing, nor do we use it for training our AI models without your explicit consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
